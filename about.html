About me

Hi, I’m Jhilik Dass, a passionate Frontend Developer & Creative Designer. I love turning ideas into clean, modern, and user-friendly websites. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS, I specialize in building responsive, interactive, and visually appealing web experiences. I enjoy crafting smooth animations with GSAP and designing intuitive UI/UX prototypes using Figma and Canva. Currently, I’m expanding my skills by learning React.js and SCSS to build more dynamic and scalable projects. My goal is to create pixel-perfect, responsive designs that not only look great but also deliver a seamless user experience across all devices.
